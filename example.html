Our Mission

FOCUS provides a platform to explore various fields of jobs and majors, including STEM, Business and Management, Medical and Healthcare, Arts and Humanities, Social Sciences, Education, Law and Public Policy, Trades and Vocational Careers, Information Technology, Hospitality and Tourism, Non-Profit, and Social Services.

STEM

Explore Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) and discover a wide range of career opportunities in these innovative fields.

Business and Management

Learn about careers in Business and Management, from entrepreneurship and finance to marketing and leadership roles in diverse industries.

Social Sciences

Social Sciences include psychology, sociology, economics, and political science. These fields help us understand people and society.

Law and Public Policy

Discover careers in law, public policy, and criminal justice. These fields shape how we live, resolve disputes, and govern society.

Information Technology

Information Technology offers paths in cybersecurity, network administration, and cloud computing. There is a role for everyone in IT.

Medical and Healthcare

Explore careers in Medical and Healthcare, from nursing and therapy to research and administration, making a difference in people's lives.

Arts and Humanities

Arts and Humanities include literature, history, philosophy, and visual arts. These fields inspire creativity and cultural understanding.

Education

Education careers range from teaching and administration to curriculum design. Find the path that matches your interests and goals.

Trades &Vocational Careers

Trades and Vocational careers include electrician, plumber, technician, and construction roles. These fields offer hands-on, rewarding work.

Hospitality and Tourism

Hospitality and Tourism careers include hotel management, travel planning, and culinary arts. These fields focus on service and experiences.

Non-Profit and Social Services

Non-Profit and Social Services careers involve supporting individuals and communities, managing organizations, and driving positive change.
