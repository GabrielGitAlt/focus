Frequently Asked Questions

Thank you for reaching out with your questions! These are some common questions people have asked us in person and from our feedback forms. Here are some answers to the questions provided. Feel free to reach out to us with any questions through our "Contact" form.

At FOCUS, we are dedicated to empowering school students by providing them with the resources and information to make informed decisions about their future career paths. Our platform serves as a bridge between students and professionals, offering valuable insights and guidance to help their academic and professional journeys. Join us today and take the first step towards a successful future!

We would love to have you volunteer or become part of our organization! Please visit our website's contact page and fill out the form or email us at focusnonprofit24@gmail.com. We will get back to you with more information about the opportunities available.

As a non-profit organization, we are committed to utilizing all our profits towards expanding our reach to other communities and enhancing the quality of materials for our hands-on activities during breakout sessions. We believe that this will enable us to make a greater impact and achieve our mission of serving the community.

At our organization, we hold diversity and inclusion as crucial values. Our aim is to establish a warm and inclusive atmosphere for all members of our community.

Yes, in fact, FOCUS has been an established non-profit organization since 8/28/2024.

Additional funding would allow us to expand our project's scope and potentially achieve even greater outcomes. By being able to reach a broad array of people from all over the nation, we can provide teens with college and career guidance by connecting them with mentors, more immersive activities, and improve speakers and lectures.

We utilize Google Sheets to monitor and handle our budget line items, keeping track of all incoming and outgoing profits. Our sponsor, WPS, has generously pledged up to $1,500 to support our project. We are actively seeking additional sponsors to help us expand our reach to other communities, and donations through our website are greatly appreciated. Our budget will be allocated towards printing posters, booking venues, providing catering for our sessions, creating merchandise (tote bags, stickers, shirts, pins), and offering gift cards to our speakers.

We take into account the feedback provided by our participants through our website's feedback. This helps us enhance our events, website, speakers, and activities to make them more engaging and enjoyable for our audience in the future.
